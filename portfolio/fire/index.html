Context

ENGR 2330: Introduction to Mechanical Prototyping (MechProto)

MechProto gives students an introduction to basic fabrication and design techniques for engineering prototypes. Over one semester, teams built three separate robots using three different methods: subtractive machining, sheetmetal, and 3D printing. A stock electronics control package and other basic components (wheels, steering arms, etc.) were also provided. Each robot had the same task: extinguish a raging campfire as quickly as possible. A $50 budget was provided to each team for the purchasing of unqiue parts or materials.
