Start by clicking Add Data to import your data into the repository. The repository is where you store all your data and save the work you do in RapidMiner. Data in the repository is the starting point for building your analytics processes. Next

Processes are made from operators. Each operator has a specific function like loading data into the process, replacing missing values in the data, or building a predictive model. Data flows between operators by connecting the output port of one to the input port of another. Next

To see the results of an operator, connect its output port to the results port (on the right side of the canvas), then click the Run button in the toolbar. Next

The Results View displays the results of the operator you connected to the results port, along with statistics and configurable charts. If you connected multiple operators, their outputs are listed as tabs at the top of the Results View. Next

Click on the Design View to get back to and enhance your process. You can change the settings of the selected operator in the Parameter panel on the right. When adding more operators, make sure again you connect the final operator to the results port. Back to Step 1
