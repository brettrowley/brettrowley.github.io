WhereTo?

A mobile application concept targeted at inexperienced users of public transportation.

Background

This concept started life as a team project in Olin's introductory interface design course. I redesigned the mockups to reflect mobile design trends and my improved visual design skills. The goal of this demo was to learn the CSS/jQuery skills needed to build a dynamic prototype from scratch.

Course Project Site

Features

Quick

WhereTo takes 30% fewer taps to get directions compared to Google Maps.

Light

WhereTo shows just enough information to get from A to B - no distractions.

Clever

WhereTo uses Predictive Analytics to take the uncertainty out of public transit.
